ABOUT

For my capstone project, I developed a web application that utilizes two NASA APIs, Astronomy Picture of the Day (APoD) and Mars Rover, which is a robotic vehicle that captures photographs of Mars' surface. My app dynamically fetches and displays images of celestial phenomenons. My project also showcases my own moon photography, displayed just above, seamlessly blending it with the NASA-provided content.
This dual approach project not only highlights the wonder of space through official NASA imagery but also provides an opportunity to display the beauty and complexity of astrophotograpy, while demonstrating creativity, media presentation and a practical application comprised of HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

ABOUT

For my capstone project, I developed a web application that utilizes two NASA APIs, Astronomy Picture of the Day (APoD) and Mars Exploration Rover images. Mars Rover is a robotic spacecraft vehicle that was rocketed to Mars specifically to search for geological clues regarding environmental conditions by capturing raw images of Mars' surface. My app dynamically fetches and displays images of celestial phenomenons with the option to hypothetically 'Subscribe' to receive a NASA daily APoD image via an entered email address.
My project also showcases my own moon photography, displayed just above, seamlessly blending it with the NASA-provided content. This dual approach project not only highlights the wonder of space through official NASA imagery but also provides an opportunity to display the beauty and complexity of astrophotograpy, while demonstrating media presentation, creativity, and a practical application comprised of HTML, CSS/Bootstrap, and Javascript.
